Welcome to

Caly Hair Styling

Alina was born in Focsani, Romania, were she started hairdressing at the age of 17(1999). She moved to London in 2003 at the age of 22 and she started working in 2005 in a Queensway, Bayswater based salon (George G Salon). After being a mum in 2006 she has relocated her work in a Baker Street based salon, Marylebone (Letitia Haute Coiffure). From 2015 she has joined HOB Elstree working as a Master Colorist.

Alina qualified for NVQ Level 2 & 3 at Barnet College (also qualified in PTLLS) from 2008 to 2010. Trained by Loreal Professionnel in London in colour (covering colour basis and colour correction principles). Trained in 2015 by Wella Koleston & HOB Academy in Camden Town in colour principles by Wella.

Throughout the years, the passion for hairdressing, especially colour was inspired by London’s diversity of cultures & nationalities which made the job full of interesting possibilities. Colouring hair means complementing the client’s facial tribe, natural undertones and is allowing Alina to create a tailored service for every need, want & lifestyle. Having had access to great training, with hard work, patience, perseverance, effective communication, listening skills, passion for hairdressing and being part of fantastic salon teams, Alina is confidently able to offer you a great variety of hairdressing services.
